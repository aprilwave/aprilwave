# Design Context

## Users

Primary audience: All of the above — indie game developers, film/TV producers, brands & agencies, and other musicians seeking music production, composition, sound design, and game audio services.

Users discover this portfolio when seeking a versatile, skilled music professional for creative projects. They land on the site curious, browsing portfolios to find someone whose work resonates and whose process feels trustworthy.

## Brand Personality

**Ethereal, cinematic, warm**

The name "Aprilwave" evokes something organic yet vast — a wave that arrives in spring, full of potential. This isn't a sterile production service; it's an artist who crafts immersive sonic experiences. The vibe isotherial, cinematic (large-scale, emotional, storytelling), but grounded in warmth (approachable, collaborative, human).

## Aesthetic Direction

- **Theme**: Dark only — aligns with the current ethereal, cinematic vibe and fits the "studio" context (creatives often work in dark environments, late at night; dark backgrounds make audio/visual portfolios feel immersive and polished)
- **Tone**: Mysterious yet inviting — the interface should feel like stepping into a creative space where exploration is rewarded
- **Key differentiator**: The site itself feels like a piece of content — intentional motion, subtle texture, no generic templates

## Design Principles

1. **Sound is visible** — leverage the existing waveform/music visualizers; the site should feel like you're interacting with music even without audio playing
2. **Warmth through texture** — avoid sterile surfaces; use subtle grain, soft glows, and organic shapes (blobs, curves) to convey the human, handcrafted nature of the work
3. **Mystery invites exploration** — don't show everything at once; use progressive disclosure, hover reveals, and layered content that rewards curiosity
4. **Professional gravitas** — while ethereal, the work is serious; showcase expertise (10+ years, multiple disciplines) without feeling corporate
5. **Motion as emotion** — the animations should feel fluid and intentional, like a well-produced track — not bouncy or flashy

## Technical Context

- **Framework**: React + Vite + Tailwind CSS
- **Routing**: wouter
- **Animation**: Framer Motion (already in use)
- **UI Components**: Radix UI primitives
- **Fonts in use**: DM Sans (body), Outfit (display), Audiowide (brand)
- **Existing color palette**: Dark navy/charcoal background with warm peach/coral primary and pale periwinkle secondary — already well-aligned with the ethereal, warm aesthetic
